Did you know? Malaysia's forests store 2.3 billion tonnes of carbon. The Permanent Reserved Forests (PRFs) represent Malaysia's commitment to sustainable forest management, covering over 12.8 million hectares of protected land.

The Forest-Air Connection: Healthy forests act as natural air purifiers, filtering pollutants and producing oxygen. When forests are lost, we lose this natural filtration system. Seasonal patterns correlate with forest fire seasons and agricultural burning periods, particularly during the dry season from June to September.
